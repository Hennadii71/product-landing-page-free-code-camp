Flagman Orbion is a series of universal spinning rods of light and medium classes. All models of the series are distinguished by rigidity and quick action. When carrying out, the upper quarter of the blank mostly works, but when weighing, almost the entire blank is included in the work, which effectively works out the jerks of the cut fish. Even the lightest model of the Orbion series is a fairly stiff rod, which is perfect for uneven, particularly jerky lines. The convenience of their performance is also facilitated by the excellent balance and low inertia of all models. Thanks to the high-modulus 30T carbon and the use of UTW technology, as well as the laconic spread handle, which ensures excellent contact between the palm and the blank, the rods have excellent tactile sensitivity. Light passing rings on the upper knee perfectly relieve the upper body. All Flagman Orbion rods are multi-purpose and can handle the vast majority of baits and fishing techniques.
